Airfield Walkers

About

There is a zombie outbreak! Everywhere you go, you see zombies walking around and enemy planes flying trough the sky. You try to get away, but you keep getting attacked!!

There are too many zombies, and you can't fight them all! So to survive, you come up with a plan to get to your plane at the end of the airport, so you can fly away!

Before getting to your plane, you need to gather fuel, because the gas tank of your get-away plane is empty! You can find them while running trough the army basis. But be careful, sometimes you need to sneak past zombies and hide from the planes to get to the fuel!

If you do come across zombies, that you can't avoid, you need to fight them! But be cautious... Every zombie you fight, makes you lose health!

Also, look out for enemy planes that you see flying around & don't get hit by them! These planes also make you lose health. And if you run out of health... then you lose!!

But don't worry! You can find food, such as apples, while getting to your plane... These apples make you gain health, so you can fight more zombies! Good luck!!
